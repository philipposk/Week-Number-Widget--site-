Week Number Widget

A clean, minimal widget that shows the current week number on your home and lock screen.

What it does

Week Number Widget follows the ISO 8601 standard by default (weeks start on Monday) and supports dark and light mode so you always know exactly which week of the year you are in at a glance.

Platforms

Built natively for both:

  • Android – Kotlin, Android Studio, home & lock screen widget.
  • iOS – SwiftUI + WidgetKit, iOS 17+ home screen widget.
